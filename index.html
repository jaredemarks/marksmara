Musat, Ilona Nastase, Bianca Olteanu, Ana-Maria Predut, Valentine

“They walk by the grass and they look at the grass. They look at the sky.”

Is the car a specter of a primal human dream or an invasive species which has evolved
alongside us? Enter the ghost. Exit the ghost.

Eerie promises of an automated utopia loom and still here we are, haunted by petrivores. Re-enter the ghost. Are we there yet?

Mara Ploscaru and Jared Marks (RO/US/DE) are multidisciplinary artists whose work spans video, sculpture, installation and performance. Trained in visual arts and dance, respectively, they blend their knowledges to morph between mediums. Their practice addresses conceptual utopias and playful correlations across various fields: technology, ecology, philosophy, pop culture and sci- fi.
